The seder's grab-bag is a resource for sed users. I collect scripts, executables, HOWTO's, tips and all kinds of stuff, and post them for all and sundry to use.

The seder's grab-bag is also home to seders, an open, informal mailing list which discusses sed and friends.

To be really useful, the Grab-bag needs regular input. If have any scripts, texts, tips, reviews, executables or source you would like to publish, I would like to hear from you. Please email all contributions (or URLs) and feedback to me. Your work will be credited, and a link to your page included if you wish. All scripts on these pages are in the public domain.
