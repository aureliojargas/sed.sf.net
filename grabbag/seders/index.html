seders


seders was created in July '96 by Al Aab to discuss sed and promote its use. It has grown from a handful of messages per week to an active, mature and well-attended list now.

It is currently hosted by a Yahoo! Groups server.  Its home page is at http://groups.yahoo.com/group/sed-users/; go there to register.
